About

I create work through movement, pressure, colour, and material. Some pieces are sharp and controlled. Others are loose, heavy, and textured. I like work that feels alive, not perfect.

Each piece is built by hand and pushed until it has its own energy.

Commissions

Commission pricing depends on size, materials, detail, and timeline. Simple and fast pieces can be kept lean. Slower, heavier pieces with more depth and finish are priced higher.

Send the wall size, room photos, rough colour direction, and budget range.
